# Code of Conduct

We want warmbly-js to be a welcoming, productive project for everyone who uses or contributes to it.
This document explains what we expect and how to raise concerns. It is written specifically for this
project.

## Our expectations

When taking part in this project, whether in issues, pull requests, discussions, or any other space,
please:

- Be respectful and assume good intent.
- Keep feedback about the code and the problem, not the person.
- Welcome newcomers and help them get oriented.
- Accept that people have different experience levels, time zones, and priorities.
- Disagree honestly and move on once a decision is made.

Please do not:

- Harass, insult, or demean others.
- Make unwelcome personal or sexual remarks.
- Share other people's private information without permission.
- Derail discussions in bad faith or repeatedly ignore maintainer decisions.

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces and to public situations where someone is representing the
project, such as posting from an official account or speaking as a maintainer.

## Reporting

If you see or experience behavior that breaks these expectations, please email
<team@warmbly.com>. Reports are read by the maintainers and kept confidential. Include what
happened, where, and any links or context that help us understand the situation.

We will review every report, respond as promptly as we reasonably can, and decide on a fair response.
Possible responses range from a private reminder to a temporary or permanent ban from project spaces,
depending on severity and history.

Maintainers who do not uphold this code in good faith may face the same consequences as anyone else.
